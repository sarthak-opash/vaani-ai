Urban Spice Bistro & Rooftop Lounge - Business Knowledge Base

Business Overview: Urban Spice Bistro & Rooftop Lounge is a modern
multi-service restaurant located in the heart of Surat, Gujarat, India.
We provide dining, reservations, home delivery, and event hosting
services. Our goal is to deliver high-quality food, excellent
hospitality, and a seamless digital experience through our AI assistant
and online platforms.

Facilities: Indoor dining, Outdoor seating, Rooftop lounge, Private

Cuisine: Indian, Chinese, Italian, Continental, Fusion dishes.

Popular Dishes: Paneer Butter Masala, Biryani, Veg Manchurian, Pasta
Alfredo, Wood-fired Pizza, Hakka Noodles, Tandoori Platter, Garlic
Bread, Chocolate Lava Cake.

Table Reservations: Customers can book tables using the AI assistant,
website, mobile app, or phone call. Required details include name, date,
time, number of guests, and seating preference such as indoor, outdoor,
or rooftop.

Home Delivery: Delivery available within a 10 km radius. Estimated
delivery time: 30 to 45 minutes. Real-time order tracking and
contactless delivery available.

Event Hosting: Birthday parties, Corporate meetings, Anniversaries,
Small celebrations. Event bookings must be made at least 24 hours in
advance.

Payment Methods: Cash, Credit Cards, Debit Cards, UPI, Online Wallets,
Net Banking. Supported apps include Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and
Amazon Pay.

Loyalty Program: Customers earn reward points on every purchase and can
redeem them for discounts and special offers.

Offers: Weekend discounts, Combo meals, Festive deals, Happy hour
specials, Corporate offers.

Hygiene and Safety: Strict sanitation practices, fresh ingredients,
trained staff, regular kitchen cleaning, and compliance with health
regulations.

Customer Support: Available during business hours through AI assistant,
